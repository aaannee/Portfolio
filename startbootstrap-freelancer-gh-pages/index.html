Leveraging my knowledge of the music industry, I guide Noah's career, secure opportunities, and provide unwavering support. Together, we form a dynamic team with a shared vision of making a lasting impact.

Noah Alejandre is a rising independent artist whose music speaks volumes about the struggles and experiences of young people today. Hailing from the Philippines, Noah has made a name for himself as a singer-songwriter with a unique and dynamic sound that blends elements of indie pop with a modern twist. Drawing from his own life and the experiences of those around him, Noah's music is heartfelt honest, and raw, with lyrics that capture the highs and lows of life in the 21st century It wasn't until he was 21 that he decided to pursue music full-time, and since then he has been making waves in the indie music scene. Despite his young age, Noah has already achieved great success in the music industry. In 2020, he and his band were crowned the Grand Champions of the PhilPop Songwriting Competition, beating out more seasoned performers with their bilingual pop hit "Suyo." Today, Noah continues to push boundaries and challenge conventions with his music, fearlessly exploring new sounds and styles to create a truly unique and unforgettable sound Whether he's performing live or recording in the studio, his songs are sure to leave a lasting impression on anyone who hears them, telling untold narratives of love, anxiety, and uncertainty, and giving a voice to those who may not otherwise have one.
